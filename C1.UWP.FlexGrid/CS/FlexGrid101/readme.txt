FlexGrid101 for UWP

Shows samples of the Grid control.

Shows the following samples

	- ColumnDefinitions
	- SelectionModes
	- EditConfirmation
	- EditingForm
	- ConditionalFormatting
	- CustomCells
	- Grouping
	- RowDetails
	- CollectionView
	- FullTextFilter
	- ColumnLayout
	- StarSizing
	- CellFreezing
	- CustomMerging
	- Unbound
	- CustomAppearance
	- NewRow
	- Financial
	- CustomSortIcon
